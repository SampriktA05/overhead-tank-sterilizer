IN-SITU OVERHEAD TANK STERILIZER
About the Project

i-OTS is an overhead tank sterilizer based on UV technology which kills or deactivates the bacteria and algae which are very hazardous causing many fungal infections and diseases. This sterilizer uses the solar power to charge and spread the UV light at specific time span set-up by us based on data observed.


Working

Our product is an UV-C-based disinfection systems designed for chemical-free water and process media in storage tanks and tanks. i-OTS is used in closed tanks with static and fluctuating levels. It is often used for optimal UV radiation coverage.i-OTS irradiates water and tank walls with UV-C radiation, inactivating existing microorganisms and germs, and suppressing further microbial growth. these UV systems are a safe and reliable solution for disinfection and also sterilizes the entire container interior and forms an effective barrier against bacterial contamination via container ventilation. UV-C radiation inactivates microorganisms based on the interaction of electromagnetic wavelengths with DNA, making them vulnerable to UVC radiation. If necessary, disinfectants like hydrogen peroxide or ozone can be combined with UV-C radiation for photochemical decomposition without harmful residues.
